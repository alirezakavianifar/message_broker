About Data Retention
What gets deleted?
  • Delivered messages with delivered_at older than the retention period
  • Failed messages with created_at older than the retention period
What is preserved?
  • Messages still queued or processing
  • Recent delivered/failed messages within the retention period
  • All audit logs (never deleted by cleanup)
  • All user and client records
Tip: For automated cleanup, configure a cron job to call the stored procedure sp_cleanup_old_messages on a regular schedule.
